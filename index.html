Welcome to Woofmail.org!

Woofmail is an online email service offered by PhoenixNet-Labs.
We offer users a unique, furry-managed communications experience, at no-cost!

Users can receive emails directly to their mailbox, or even opt-in to forward them to other email providers, like Gmail, Yahoo, or any other provider.

Registration is currently manually approved at this time, this is to prevent abuse, and to maintain a good reputation with major email providers. Please visit the registration tab for more information.

Terms of Service:

We're going to put this into plain-english so that the rules are very clear.
Failure to abide by these rules, will result in account termination, and deletion of user data.

Please do not distribute illegal content via Woofmail, this includes content that may be legal in your jurisdiction but may not be in ours. This service is hosted in the US, so please respect US law.

Please do not abuse/misuse/exploit/repurpose Woofmail for activities that may violate other providers ToS, including spam, spreading viruses, phishing, or anything alike.

Users are restricted to (1) account, with a 5GB mailbox. Aliases are available upon request. Mailbox size increases are also available upon request.

User inactivity will lead to account deletion, users who do not send any email for over 1 year will be notified of inactivity and then deleted if no response is received.

This goes without saying, please do not attempt to hack, exploit, modify, or otherwise use Woofmail in any way not intended by design.

Are you law-enforcement, legal-personnel, or a 3-letter agency interested in contacting us regarding misuse? Please reach out to abuse@woofmail.org to discuss further.

Are you having trouble accessing your email, or logging in? Please send an email to support@phoenixnet.tech.
